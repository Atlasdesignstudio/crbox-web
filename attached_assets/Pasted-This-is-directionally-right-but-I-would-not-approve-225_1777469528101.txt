This is directionally right, but I would not approve #225 yet without a few important refinements.

## What I agree with
I agree with the overall bundle:
- refine the Contáctenos page UX
- update the Llámanos phone number
- improve and reposition the form
- add the Miami office to Sucursales
- and make public-site inquiries visible inside the internal operational system, not only in email

That direction is correct.

## Key refinement 1 — do not mix general inquiries into quote_requests unless absolutely necessary
The plan currently says:
- add a request_type column to quote_requests
- or create a separate lightweight general_inquiries table if cleaner

My strong preference is:
### use a separate table for general inquiries

These are not quote requests.
They do not share the same business model as cotizaciones:
- no shipping fields
- no estimate
- no intent flow
- no package linking
- no quote response composer

So I want this to stay conceptually clean:
- same internal tool if needed
- but a separate data model

## Key refinement 2 — keep “Consultas Generales” semantically separate from cotizaciones
I do want these inquiries visible inside the internal system, but I do not want them to feel like just another status filter of solicitudes if that causes conceptual mixing.

So please make sure the internal surface reflects:
- Cotizaciones
- and Consultas Generales

as different intake types, even if they live in the same admin tool.

## Key refinement 3 — do not lose the inquiry if email fails
Please make the behavior explicit:
- if the inquiry is stored successfully in the database
- but the ventas email fails

the inquiry should still remain saved in the internal system.
Log the email failure, but do not lose the submission.

## Key refinement 4 — make the intake pattern reusable
Please design this in a way that can later be reused by:
- the Contáctenos form
- the FAQ inline form on Cómo Funciona
- and other lightweight public-site question forms

So even if this task implements it first for contacto.html, the structure should not be one-off or tightly coupled only to that page.

## UX refinements
For the page itself:

1. The three top cards should feel much more intentional and visually stronger, but still warm and simple — not overdesigned.
2. The form should feel short, modern, and low-friction.
3. Please review whether every field is truly necessary from a UX perspective.
4. Adding the Miami office should not break the balance of the branches/locations section, especially on mobile.

If you include these refinements, then the task direction looks good.
