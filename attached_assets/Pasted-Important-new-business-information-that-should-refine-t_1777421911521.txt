Important new business information that should refine the model and the next-task planning.

## New business reality for the Comprar con CRBOX path
When CRBOX buys the product on behalf of the customer, the shipment arrives in Miami under CRBOX’s name, not directly under the customer’s profile / casillero.

Because of that:
- there is no automatic way to assign that Miami package to the customer profile at that stage
- the customer will not see meaningful package progress in Mis Paquetes during that part of the flow
- the package is only assigned manually later, when CRBOX staff in Costa Rica receives it and associates it internally
- the customer is then informed that it has arrived and is ready for pickup

## Product implication
This changes how the two post-response paths should be understood:

### 1. Lo compraré por mi cuenta
This remains the path where:
- expected package
- optional tracking number
- linked_package_id
- and eventual relationship to Mis Paquetes

make the most sense.

So the expected-package / overlay-linking model is primarily for this self-purchase path.

### 2. Comprar con CRBOX
This path should not be modeled as something the customer follows through Mis Paquetes during the Miami / transit stage.

Instead, the correct customer-facing surface for this branch is:
- Mis Solicitudes as the main progress surface
- with clear manual / operational status progression
- and then a final update when the package has arrived in Costa Rica and is ready for pickup

In other words:
for the CRBOX-managed purchase branch, the customer should understand progress through solicitud states, not by expecting a package to appear in Mis Paquetes.
