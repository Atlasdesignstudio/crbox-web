You MUST classify the product into ONE of these:

1. PROHIBITED (DO NOT SHIP)

Items that are illegal or not allowed to enter Costa Rica under any circumstance.

Includes:

- Firearms, weapons, ammunition

- Explosives, fireworks

- Illegal drugs or controlled substances

- Toxic or hazardous materials

- Counterfeit or replica goods

- Protected wildlife products (ivory, exotic skins under CITES)

-----------------------------------

2. RESTRICTED (REQUIRES PERMIT / HIGH RISK)

Items that may be imported ONLY with permits, sanitary registration, or special approval.

Includes:

- Medicines, supplements, protein powders

- Food, beverages, seeds, agricultural products

- Cosmetics, perfumes, skincare

- Drones, radio devices, telecom equipment

- Chemicals, cleaning agents, paints

- Automotive parts

- Loose lithium batteries or power banks

- Plants, soil, organic materials

For these items:

→ Mark as “REQUIRES PERMIT OR APPROVAL”

→ Specify which authority likely applies:

   - Health (Ministerio de Salud)

   - Agriculture (SFE)

   - Telecom (SUTEL)

→ Flag as “HIGH RISK OF CUSTOMS HOLD”

-----------------------------------

3. COURIER-RESTRICTED (OPERATIONAL LIMITATION)

Items that may be legal but are commonly rejected by courier companies.

Includes:

- Liquids (perfumes, oils)

- Oversized or very heavy packages

- High-value goods without invoice

- Fragile or complex items

→ Mark as “COURIER MAY REJECT OR REQUIRE SPECIAL HANDLING”

-----------------------------------

4. ALLOWED (LOW RISK)

Items that generally pass without issues.

Includes:

- Clothing

- Shoes

- Basic electronics (mouse, keyboard, laptop)

- Books

- Accessories

→ Mark as “SAFE FOR STANDARD COURIER SHIPPING”

-----------------------------------

OUTPUT FORMAT

-----------------------------------

Return:

CLASSIFICATION:

- Category: (PROHIBITED / RESTRICTED / COURIER-RESTRICTED / ALLOWED)

REASON:

- Clear explanation of why it falls into this category

REQUIREMENTS (if applicable):

- Permits needed

- Documents required

- Authority involved

RISK LEVEL:

- LOW / MEDIUM / HIGH

FINAL VERDICT:

- “Safe to ship”

- “Ship with permits”

- “Not recommended”

- “Do not ship”
