### What I want for the CRBOX-purchase path right now
When the customer chooses:
- Comprar con CRBOX
- Lo compraré por mi cuenta
- No continuar

that intent should absolutely be visible to CRBOX sales/admin.

But for the Comprar con CRBOX path, I do not want a heavy automated fulfillment workflow yet.

Instead, the near-term model should be:

- customer selects Comprar con CRBOX
- sales/admin is notified or can clearly see that intent
- sales/admin can manually progress the solicitud through clear states such as:
  - pendiente_compra_crbox
  - pendiente_confirmacion_pago_cliente
  - pagado_por_cliente
  - comprado
- the customer sees understandable progress in the portal
- once the item is marked as comprado, the customer receives a clear signal/message that CRBOX has already purchased it

That is enough for now.
