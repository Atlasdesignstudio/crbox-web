POST `https://clients.crbox.cr/authtoken`

Body:
- `grant_type=password`
- `username={email}`
- `password={password}`

Content-Type:
`application/x-www-form-urlencoded`

Confirmed success response:
```json id="0s4y1a"

  "access_token": "...",
  "token_type": "bearer",
  "expires_in": 86399
}
```

`Authorization: Bearer {token}`

- store token
- store expires_at
