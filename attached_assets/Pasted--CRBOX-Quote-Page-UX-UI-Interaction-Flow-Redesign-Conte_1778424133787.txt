# CRBOX Quote Page — UX/UI + Interaction Flow Redesign

## Context

We need to improve the current `cotizar` experience. The page already has a strong hero/search interaction, but once the user submits a product, the experience becomes too form-like, visually cramped, and inconsistent depending on whether the product was typed manually, selected from a category chip, or clicked from “Populares hoy”.

The goal is to make the quote flow feel modern, friendly, fluid, and premium — closer to the experience quality of Apple, Shopify, or a modern product configurator — while keeping the CRBOX brand language: warm, clear, orange-forward, approachable, and non-technical.

This should not feel like a traditional form. It should feel like a guided product quote builder where the user adds one or multiple products, receives friendly AI-assisted guidance, understands the estimated taxes/shipping in a visual way, and can either add another product or submit the full quote request.

---

# Main Objective

Redesign and refine the quote experience so that:

1. Product analysis/loading feels smooth and friendly.
2. The AI/product response has clear visual hierarchy instead of appearing as one long paragraph.
3. Product details are easier to edit, correct, and enrich.
4. The user can add multiple products naturally.
5. Added products remain visible as clean, collapsible product cards.
6. The layout uses more of the viewport on desktop instead of feeling like a narrow centered form.
7. The experience remains fully responsive and excellent on mobile.
8. All entry points behave consistently: manual search, arrow click, Enter key, category chips, and “Populares hoy” links.
9. All financial/tax information is clearly framed as estimated/orientative, not final.
10. The final flow clearly separates “add another product” from “submit quote request”.

---

# Problems to Fix

## 1. Broken/clipped loading state

When the user enters a product and presses Enter or clicks the arrow, the lower module starts loading but the inner orange analysis card appears clipped/cut off during the transition.

### Required fix

- Fix the layout/height/overflow issue so the orange analysis module never appears visually cut off.
- Avoid sudden jumps, clipped corners, or content being hidden during loading.
- Use proper container min-height, transition timing, and overflow rules.
- The loading state should reserve enough space for the incoming product card.

---

## 2. Improve the loading experience

The current loading state feels too abrupt. We need something more friendly and contextual.

### Required behavior

When a product starts processing, show a warm loading module such as:

- “Analizando tu producto…”
- “Buscando la categoría más probable…”
- “Estimando impuestos y tipo de envío…”
- “Preparando una cotización orientativa…”

Use a lightweight animated state: shimmer, pulsing dots, progress steps, or small CRBOX-style assistant indicator.

### Important

Do not overcomplicate it. It should feel calm, fast, and reassuring, not like a chatbot waiting forever.

Analizando tu producto...
