### 3. Keep conditions blank when not needed

- if there are no meaningful conditions, Gemini should return conditions: ""

Please preserve and keep explicit:

- Gemini receives the confirmed shipping price and system estimate only as read-only context for drafting quality
- Gemini must never:
  - suggest the confirmed price
  - comment on whether the confirmed price is correct

  - act as the pricing engine

For the prompt, I want the drafting context passed to Gemini in clearly structured form, not loosely embedded prose.

Please include structured context such as:

- confirmed reviewed response price
- numeric difference

This should improve draft quality without turning Gemini into the pricing engine.
