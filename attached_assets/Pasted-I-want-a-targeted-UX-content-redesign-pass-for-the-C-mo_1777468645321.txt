In the “Resolvemos tus dudas” section, I do not want the current “Contáctanos para más información” style CTA as the main path anymore.

That creates unnecessary friction.

Instead, I want a small, clear question form directly in that section so the user can send their question right there.

## What I want in that area
A simple form that includes:
- Nombre
- Correo
- Pregunta
- Enviar

The idea is:
- user types their question
- submits it
- and it gets sent directly to ventas by email

This should feel:
- easy
- immediate
- low-friction
- more helpful than sending them away to a contact page or vague CTA

## Important
The form should be visually integrated into the FAQ section and feel lightweight, not like a huge enterprise form.

It should feel like:
- “Didn’t find your answer? Ask us here.”

## Submission behavior
Please wire it so that the submitted question is sent directly to the sales email / ventas flow already used elsewhere where appropriate.

If you need to reuse existing email infrastructure, do that.

I want this to reduce friction, not add a detached contact journey.
